The Sugar Factory is an innovative press agency creating hard-hitting campaigns for the creative sectors. We work alongside record labels, cultural institutions, artists studios and independent creatives.

With an emphasis on music, we are dedicated to representing boundary-pushing musicians across a wide breadth of genres from avant-garde to pop; we have a proven track record of nurturing new exciting talent as well as established stars.

Our specialist knowledge also serves the promotion of festivals, brand events, gallery openings and beyond. Working across the UK, US and EU territories, both online and print, we offer bespoke campaigns tailored to the client’s needs. We also offer brand partnership strategy as well as media training.

Counting past and current clients like Somerset House Studios and Southbank Centre, legacy labels such as Warp Records and RVNG Intl. as well as the boundary-pushing Yves Tumor and electrifying rapper Ashnikko - to name a few, founder Isis O’Regan has worked with some of the most exciting names in the business. The Sugar Factory is a boutique agency offering campaigns that are executed with passion.

Reach out to isis@tsf-pr.com to find out how we could collaborate on something unique.
